Pesto Pasta

Full of flavor and easy to make!

Pesto pasta is a great dish to make for dinner since it is full of flavor and easy to make. What makes this recipe great is that it can be eaten cold or hot! The recipe that you will follow is quick and great for beginners as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons pesto
  • salt to taste
  • ground pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lighty salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Stir in pasta and return to a boil.
  3. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occassionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  5. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes.
  6. Stir in pesto, salt and pepper until warmed through.
  7. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.

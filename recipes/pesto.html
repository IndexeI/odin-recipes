Pesto Pasta

Full of flavor and easy to make!

Pesto pasta is a great dish to make for dinner since it is full of flavor and easy to make. What makes this recipe great is that it can be eaten cold or hot! The recipe that you will follow is quick and great for beginners as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons pesto
  • salt to taste
  • ground pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
